# InnerSpace 🧠

> *Meet people where they are. Guide them to where they need to be.*

InnerSpace is an intelligent mental health coordination platform that connects individuals to the right level of support at the right time. It is not a replacement for therapy — it is the infrastructure layer that has never existed between a person realizing they're struggling and actually getting help.

---

## The Problem

Today, someone feeling anxious or burned out Googles their symptoms, lands on 47 different apps that each do one thing poorly, gets overwhelmed, and does nothing. The gap between "I think I need help" and "I am getting help" is where people fall through.

InnerSpace closes that gap.

---

## Who It's For

- **Individuals** — People experiencing stress, anxiety, burnout, or grief who need guidance but aren't in acute crisis
- **Organizations** — Schools, universities, and employers who want visibility into collective mental health trends without violating individual privacy
- **Mental Health Professionals** — Therapists who want a smarter way to receive pre-assessed, well-documented clients

---

## Core Features

### 🔍 Smart Intake Assessment
Clinically validated screeners (PHQ-9, GAD-7) delivered conversationally. Results generate a severity score that determines the user's personalized support tier. Not a form — a conversation.

### 🧭 Personalized Support Dashboard
Every user gets a home that adapts to their current mental health tier — self-guided content, peer circles, therapist matches, or crisis resources — and evolves as their data changes.

### 📓 AI-Powered Journal
Private journaling where AI reflects back patterns, asks gentle follow-up questions, and helps users process their thoughts. Every entry is analyzed for sentiment and emotional themes in the background.

### 📊 Mood & Pattern Tracker
Daily 60-second check-ins that build a longitudinal picture over time. Users see their own patterns — what days are hardest, what correlates with low mood, how they've trended over 30/60/90 days.

### 👥 Peer Support Circles
Anonymized, moderated group spaces organized by shared experience — anxiety, grief, burnout, academic pressure. AI-assisted moderation runs on every message in real time.

### 🔎 Therapist Directory & Smart Matching
AI matches users to providers based on their assessment profile, preferences, location, language, and availability — not just keyword search.

### 🚨 Crisis Detection & Escalation
The most critical feature. A two-layer system (keyword matching + AI scoring) monitors journal entries, check-ins, and circle messages for crisis signals and responds in calibrated layers — never abrupt, always human.

### 🏫 Organization Dashboard
Schools and employers see anonymized aggregate mental health trends — never individual data. Trend lines, high-risk period detection, and resource utilization rates.

### 📋 Clinical Summary Export
Users generate a structured PDF of their mood history, journal themes, and assessment scores to share with any new provider. No more starting from zero.
